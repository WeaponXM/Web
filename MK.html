Overview

Developed and published by Midway, its release in 1992 made it the first fighting game to include a block button and involve violence.

Features/Game Modes

Single Player: In this mode, you fight against a CPU. There are a total of 11 matches - 7 are 1 on 1 battles, 3 are 1 on 2 battles, and the last one is a boss battle.
Multiplayer: In this mode, you fight against another player.
Fatalities: Each character has their own unique fatality which triggers when inputting the right combination at the right distance after the enemy loses all of their HP. There is also a special fatality when playing on the pit stage that triggers if an uppercut is performed at the end of the fight.
