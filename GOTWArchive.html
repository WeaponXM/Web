Tekken 8

Released Thursday January 26 for the PS5, Windows, and Xbox Series X/S, this game features 32 new and returning characters, each possessing their own unique fighting style and contributing to the game's story.

Killer Instinct (1994)

Released Wednesday February 21 for the Nintendo Switch, this is a re-release of a classic fighting game that introduced the two life bar system, auto doubles (pressing a sequence of buttons to make characters automatically perform combos on opponents), and combo breakers (special defensive moves that can interrupt combos).

Star Wars: Unlimited

Released Friday March 7, this is a card game that features a 50-card deck of iconic Star Wars characters.

Princess Peach: Showtime

Released Friday March 22 for the Nintendo Switch, this Mario spinoff features Princess Peach as the lead character and comes with new power ups for players to enjoy.

House Flipper 2

Released April 10 for Xbox and Playstation, this game provides satisfaction by allowing the player to renovate houses and even build their own using their creativity.

Grounded

Released April 15 for the Nintendo Switch, Playstation 4, and Playstation 5, this is a survival and exploration game where the player experiences everything from the perspective of a child who has been shrunken down to the size of a bug.

Crossy Road Castle

Released Thursday April 25 for more platforms, this game involves an endless tower climb that can be played with friends.

Pac-Man Mega Tunnel Battle: Chomp Champs

Released on Wednesday May 8 for Xbox, Playstation, PC and the Nintendo Switch, this is an online version of Pac-Man where you can compete against other players to see who can eat the most.
