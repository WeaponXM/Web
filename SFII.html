Overview

Released in 1991 by Capcom, it is credited with popularizing the fighting genre during the 1990s, inspiring a flurry of new fighting games, and heavily impacted competitive video gaming (gaming tournaments).

Features/Game Modes

Single Player: In this mode, you fight against a CPU. There are up to 10 rounds per match (4 max in later versions).
Multiplayer: In this mode, you fight against another player. A second player can also start a round at any time during a single player match.
Character Variety: Each character has their own distint fighting style and special moves.
