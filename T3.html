Overview

This game was originally developed and released in 1997 by Namco, and its graphics, animations, and faster/fluid gameplay has led to it being considered one of the greatest fighting games on the original PlayStation console.

Features/Game Modes

Single Player: In this mode, you battle against a CPU.
Multiplayer: In this mode, two players fight against each other
Tekken Ball: This mode is a 1v1 game of beach volleyball where kicks and punches charge charge the ball up and knock it to the opponent's side. There are three balls to choose from, and they each deal a different amount of damage to the opponent.
Tekken Force: This mode is a side scrolling mini-game where you can fight against enemies in four stages. At the end of each stage is a boss character.
