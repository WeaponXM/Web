Overview

Developed by HAL Laboratory and released in 1999 for the Nintendo 64, Super Smash Bros. popularized crossover fighting games and its success led to future installments in the series.

Features/Game Modes

1P (Single Player): In this mode, you choose your lives as well as the difficulty of the fights. There are 11 stages and 3 bonus bonus stages (one optional after every third match) that add to a total score. At the end, you must fight the boss Master Hand.
Versus (Multiplayer): This mode is the traditional mutiplayer battle, however you can set the rules and add up to 4 players/CPU to the fight.
No Smash Balls: Unlike the future installments of Super Smash Bros., there are no ultimate moves that the characters can use.
