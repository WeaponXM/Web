Overview

Developed by Spike and published by Bandai Namco, Dragon Ball Z: Budokai Tenkaichi is an anime fighting game released in 2005 as a PlayStation 2 exclusive and involves a third person camera perspective as well as a bar that you have to charge up in order to use special moves.
