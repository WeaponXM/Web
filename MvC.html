Overview

Marvel Vs Capcom: Clash of Super Heroes debuted in arcades in 1998. While not technically the first installment of the series, it introduced characters from both the Marvel and the Capcom universes and was one of the first video games to feature tag team battles.

Features/Game Modes

Arcade: In this mode, the player must defeat teams of CPU enemies and the final boss, Onslaught.
Versus: In this mode, two players can fight with handicaps and characters as well as on a stage of their choosing.
Training: This mode allows a player to adjust their settings and practice their moves.
Survival: In this mode, the player fights waves of enemies with a time limit and health that carries over each round.
Cross Fever: This is a mode where four players simultaneously compete in a 2-on-2 match.
Cross Over: This mode involves tag team gameplay.
