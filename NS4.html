Overview

Developed by CyberConnect2 and published by Bandai Namco, Naruto Ultimate Ninja Storm 4 is an anime fighting game released in 2016. Using the regular fighting game systems of the previous systems and including over 100 beloved characters from the Naruto series, it became one of the best-selling fighting games of all time.
